Cloudiness (%) vs. City Latitude



Based on the plot, there is seemingly no linear relationship between latitude and the percentage of cloudiness. This is unsurprising considering that the formation of clouds is dependent on daily temperatures, a variable with mulitple affecting factors. There is an indirect relationship considering latitude does play a role in a city's climate and therefore temperature but it is worth noting that clouds are weather-based as opposed to climate-based.
