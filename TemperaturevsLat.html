Temperature (F) vs. City Latitude



As expected, there is a relationship between a city's latitude and its temperature. Per our plot, we see that temperatures are higher where their location is closer to the equator. This is primarily due to the angle at which Earth is rotating on its axis- the centre is "closest" to the sun resulting in higher temperatures. Temperatures are higher in the 20 thru 60 degree latitude range which is potentially a result of seasonal differences.
